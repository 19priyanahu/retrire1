THE MONEYVESTA METHOD

How We Build Your Retirement Plan

Our framework blends numbers, behaviour, lifestyle needs and long term wealth strategy.

01

Life and Lifestyle Discovery

We understand the lifestyle you want in retirement. Travel, healthcare needs, home plans, hobbies, responsibilities and preferred living standards.

02

Retirement Corpus Calculation

We calculate your retirement number based on current expenses, lifestyle inflation, healthcare inflation, longevity assumptions and future responsibilities. This gives you a clear target.

03

EPF, NPS and Existing Investments Review

We analyse how your existing assets contribute to your retirement goal. This avoids duplication and helps you understand gaps.

04

Investment Strategy for Retirement

Your retirement portfolio is built with the right mix of:

  • long term compounding assets
  • stability focused investments
  • inflation beating components
  • low volatility income streams
05

SIP and Contribution Planning

We help you understand how much you need to invest: monthly, annually and through variable income like bonuses and ESOPs.

06

Post Retirement Income Strategy

We structure: systematic withdrawals, annuity needs, safety buckets, medical buffers and liquidity planning so your money lasts as long as you need it to.

07

Annual Review and Adjustments

Life changes. Market cycles shift. Your retirement plan evolves through yearly reviews.
